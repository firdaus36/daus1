My Diary

December 23, 2023

Last Saturday stands out as one of the best days in my memory. Though it was tiring, the effort was entirely worthwhile when I came to pick you up. Your beauty was striking, and the surprise of our matching outfits added a charming touch. Despite the initial awkwardness, I believe that over time, we grew more comfortable and understanding with each other.

December 23, 2023

Our time at the theme park was special; my gaze was constantly drawn to you. I confess, I was anxious about your comfort throughout. After the theme park, you assisted me in the hotel, and we shared moments in the car, engaging in the same delightful chit-chat. I truly treasured our conversation.

December 23, 2023

Later, at the mall, our plan to eat there became a spontaneous exploration as we wandered without a specific dining choice in mind. Eventually, we settled on shawarma, and during that meal, we engaged in some captivating discussions. I found myself wishing to freeze that moment in time. We continued our chit-chat, enjoying shawarma in the car.

December 23, 2023

I reluctantly had to bring you back home. I left with the hope of spending more precious time with you in the future.
